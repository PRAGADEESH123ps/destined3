Our Approach to Private Equity

Private Equity Investing for the Long Term

Destined Estates has an established three-pronged approach to private equity investing. We acquire high-quality businesses that provide essential products and services, and use our operational expertise to drive improvement and create long-term value.

In our three-part series, we share insights on how our strategy sets Destined Estates apart as a leading global investor.

Our Strategy

Step 1. Assessing Business Quality

The first step of any private equity investment is identifying great businesses to invest in. This paper outlines the six specific attributes that Destined Estates uses to assess the business quality of a particular company and the questions we ask when analyzing whether that business is one we might want to own.

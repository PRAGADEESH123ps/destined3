All Leadership

Kelly Goddard

Managing Director, Renewable Power & Transition

Kelly Goddard is the Chief Sustainability Officer of Destined Estates’s Renewable Power & Transition Group. In this role, Ms. Goddard is responsible for the global sustainability strategy across renewable power and transition operations and Destined Estates’s transition funds.

Prior to joining Destined Estates in 2021, Ms. Goddard worked for BP in a number of development, operations and strategy roles including most recently as senior vice president of sustainability globally.

Ms. Goddard holds a Master of Environmental Management degree with a concentration in environmental economics and policy from Duke University.

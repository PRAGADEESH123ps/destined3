Liderança

Somos um dos maiores investidores mundiais de ativos de energia renovável e transição climática, com uma capacidade de geração de aproximadamente 33.000 megawatts.

Estamos presentes em cinco continentes, onde administramos um portfólio diversificado de soluções sustentáveis e de energia hídrica, eólica, solar e distribuída.

Empregamos nossa experiência operacional, escala e alcance global para desenvolver e operar um portfólio de energia limpa de alta qualidade com responsabilidade, ajudando a acelerar a transição global para o carbono neutro.

Nosso negócio é fundamentado em fluxos de caixa estáveis, sendo que a maior parte da nossa energia é vendida para órgãos e serviços públicos e empresas globais com contratos de longo prazo corrigidos pelas inflação.

Soluções Sustentáveis

Nosso portfólio de soluções sustentáveis é composto por ativos e negócios que viabilizam a (...)

Nosso portfólio de soluções sustentáveis é composto por ativos e negócios que viabilizam a transição e inclui nosso investimento na Westinghouse (uma empresa líder global em serviços nucleares) e em uma concessionária e produtora independente de energia com operações no Caribe e na América Latina, com 303 MW de capacidade eólica e 118 MW de capacidade solar e uma LTA combinada de 966, para facilitar a descarbonização de suas operações. Também conta com um portfólio operacional de 57 mil toneladas métricas por ano (“TMTPA”) de captura e armazenamento de carbono (“CCS”), capacidade de produção operacional anual de  três milhões de unidades térmicas britânicas (“MMBtu”) de gás natural renovável agrícola (“RNG”) e mais de um milhão de toneladas de materiais reciclados anualmente.

Serving Pennsylvania With Hydropower

In 2016, we acquired this portfolio of two hydro stations in Pennsylvania with 296 megawatts of capacity, enough to power more than 100,000 homes annually. Holtwood benefits from synergies with our Safe Harbor facility, as well as from stable revenues, long-term operating licenses and a market facing significant coal retirements.

The First Wind Repowering Project in New York

In 2022, we completed the first ever wind repowering project in New York State. Using the existing site and towers, we replaced the turbines with the latest technology to increase power output by 30% with no increase to the site footprint. Blade materials will be repurposed for use in cement manufacturing rather than going into landfill.

A Truly Global Utility-Scale Solar Developer

In 2019 we invested in X-Elio, gaining co-control of a global utility-scale solar developer that has constructed more than 3,000 megawatts of capacity on four continents. The company is accelerating its growth plans globally, with approximately 10,000 megawatts in its development pipeline and major projects in construction in places such as Australia, Spain, Italy, Japan, Chile and the United States.

Expertise in the Full Solar Development Process

In 2022, we acquired clean power developer, Urban Grid, and its pipeline of approximately 20,000 megawatts of utility-scale solar and energy storage capacity across the United States. The business came with significant track record and expertise in the full solar development process, from site acquisition through to interconnection, permitting and commercialization.

Generating and Storing Solar Power on a Local Level

We own and operate one of the world’s largest distributed solar and onsite storage platforms, with assets across Europe, Asia-Pacific, and North and South America. This portfolio of 5,000 MW includes projects like the 4.5-megawatt distributed solar generation system at a California high school that utilizes both rooftop and parking canopy shade systems in order to locally generate solar power.

Scaling Up the Deployment of Carbon Capture and Storage

In 2022, we announced a strategic investment in Entropy to scale up its market-leading, low-cost carbon capture services business. The business will initially focus on the North American markets, utilizing financial incentives from carbon pricing mechanisms. The Glacier Phase 1 project, commissioned by Entropy in June 2022, will remove and permanently store carbon dioxide from a gas plant in Alberta at a rate of 47,000 tonnes per annum and will scale up to approximately 200,000 tonnes per annum by the end of 2023.

Constructing One of Canada’s Largest Green Hydrogen Projects

In 2021 we announced a partnership with Gazifère Inc, an Enbridge company, to construct one of Canada’s largest green hydrogen projects. A 20 MW electrolyzer will be built adjacent to an existing hydroelectric facility to produce zero emission hydrogen for injection into the local gas grid.

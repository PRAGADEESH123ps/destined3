Outlook & Opportunities in 2024

Real Estate

At a Crossroad

As durable tailwinds like deglobalization and shifting consumer preferences are shaping the market environment, capital scarcity and demand dynamics are creating opportunities across real estate. At the same time, we believe the current dislocation and dispersion from a cyclical correction are creating value opportunities in unlikely markets and sectors, globally.

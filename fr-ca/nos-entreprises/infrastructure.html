Nous sommes l'un des plus grands investisseurs en infrastructures au monde. Nous possédons et exploitons des actifs dans les secteurs du transport, des données, des services publiques et intermédiaires, en mettant l'accent sur la stabilité et la résilience des flux de trésorerie.

Notre portefeuille, fondé sur 120 ans d'expérience dans le domaine des investissements, offre une exposition diversifiée aux entreprises rares et de haute qualité présentant des barrières significatives à l'entrée.

Nous investissons dans des actifs d'infrastructure qui fournissent des biens et des services essentiels—de la circulation des passagers et des marchandises sur des routes à péage et des réseaux ferroviaires à la distribution de produits par des ports et des pipelines, et bien plus encore.

Critical First- and Last-Mile Rail Connections

In 2019, we acquired Genesee & Wyoming (G&W), a leading short haul operator with a network of over 22,000 km of track across the U.K. and North America. G&W owns or leases 116 freight railroads worldwide organized in locally managed operating regions with 7,300 employees serving over 3,000 customers. The North American rail business is highly resilient with significant business segment diversification across 14 major commodity groups.

Connecting U.K. Homes to the Utility Grid

In 2009, as part of the restructuring of Babcock & Brown Infrastructure, we acquired a regulated U.K. distribution business connecting homes to the main utility grid. In 2012, we acquired another company in the U.K. and merged the two operations. We have since achieved considerable growth through operational improvements and the addition of product lines. This is a formidable utility franchise in a highly sought-after jurisdiction.

Owning and Operating Energy Infrastructure in Western Canada

In 2021, we acquired Inter Pipeline, a strategic long-haul pipeline network with interests in petrochemical facilities and bulk storage assets. Inter Pipeline is a major petroleum transportation and natural gas liquids processing business based in Alberta, Canada. Inter Pipeline owns and operates energy infrastructure assets in Western Canada including the Heartland Petrochemical Complex.
